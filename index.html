PROFESSIONAL SUMMARY

What is a professional summary? A professional summary is a list about 4-5 bullet points long. It summarizes your skills, experiences and achievements as they pertain to a job opening. You can think of it as a teaser for the rest of your resume.

SKILLS

  • Communication
  • Ability to Work Under Pressure
  • Decision Making
  • Time Management
  • Self-motivation
  • Conflict Resolution
  • Leadership
  • Adaptability

WORK HISTORY

  • the applicant, and the review becomes part of the overall selection
